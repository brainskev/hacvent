## ⚠️ POTENTIAL ISSUES & MITIGATIONS

### Issue 1: "Rebate Amount Claims"

**Risk**: Google may flag claims about "$5,000+ savings"  
**Mitigation**:

- Add disclaimer: "Rebate amounts vary by location and program"
- Use range (e.g., "$2,000-$14,000 potential savings")
- Back up with data from actual rebate programs
- Test claims with Google before running ads

### Issue 2: "Contractor Partnership Claims"

**Risk**: Need to verify contractors are real and legitimate  
**Mitigation**:

- Maintain database of verified contractors
- Require license verification during onboarding
- Document contractor approval process
- Be prepared to show contractor verification to Google

### Issue 3: "Competitor Bidding"

**Risk**: Other rebate or HVAC services may bid on keywords  
**Mitigation**:

- Use negative keywords to exclude competitors
- Bid higher on brand keywords (hacvent)
- Focus on long-tail keywords with less competition
- Differentiate with unique selling points

### Issue 4: "Landing Page Relevance"

**Risk**: Landing pages may not match ad copy perfectly  
**Mitigation**:

- Create dedicated landing pages for major campaigns
- Use dynamic keyword insertion where possible
- Ensure fast load times on landing pages
- A/B test landing page variations

### Issue 5: "Lead Quality"

**Risk**: Lead volume might be high but quality low  
**Mitigation**:

- Implement lead scoring system
- Track conversion from lead to actual application
- Follow up with leads quickly (within 24 hours)
- Monitor and optimize form fields (fewer is better)
